ABOUT US

Memento is a company that was created in the year 2015 when the current partners found a gap
in the photography industry. The gap came about from the fact that clients looking for
photographers found a hard time finding their dream photographer. Well,this is where
Memento jumped in to help. We came up with a company that connects clients to their dream
photographer for any single event available and the price rates for each photographer for
each event to save time when looking for photographers and on discussing and bargaining prices.

We believe that our company is for every single one of you. Do not worry about the prices
because, let's be honest, by the end of the day we want satisafactory work but the money
also has it's role to play hence we have the best photographers in the country offering
great services at affordable and various prices.

We as the memento fraternity try everyday to live by our mission which is we capture
your moments from today as we create memories for a lifetime.
